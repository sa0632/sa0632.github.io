About Us 💛

A family, a dream, and a really good buttercream recipe.

Our Story 🧁

Started in a kitchen.
Grown with love.

Sprinkle & Co. started in 2014 when sisters Rosa and Maya Velez decided to turn their Sunday baking sessions into something more. They started with a farmers market table and 40 cookies. They sold out in 45 minutes.

Ten years later, they have a storefront on Oak Street, a team of seven, and still use their grandmother's buttercream recipe. It hasn't changed once.

They believe baked goods should make people happy — not just satisfied. Every item on the menu has to pass the "does this make you smile?" test before it goes on the shelf.
