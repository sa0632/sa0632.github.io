Our Story

Crafted with Passion & Authenticity

"Our Vietnamese Pork Banh Mi features savory pork, crisp veggies, and flavorful sauce inside a warm crunchy baguette. A quick meal that never disappoints."

At Amy's Bánh Mì, we believe that the secret to a perfect sandwich starts with the bread. That's why our baguettes are made in-house — a rare and difficult skill that our dedicated staff has perfected to bring you the truest taste of Vietnam right here in Hampton.
